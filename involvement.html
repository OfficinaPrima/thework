Explore your involvement

This is patronage in the oldest sense: backing a maker, and work meant to outlast everyone involved. You fund a technology's development, and you own a share of what it earns in the market. The work itself remains the maker's.

What it asks for is a posture: the work first, the horizon long, the risk understood and accepted. A few questions to see whether we're a fit. If we are, the next step is a conversation.
